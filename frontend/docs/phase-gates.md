# Delivery Phase Gates (Weeks 2, 6, 12)

## Gate A (End of Week 2): Quick Win Quality

### Exit Criteria
- Landing CTA ambiguity removed.
- Settings email prefill and account context shipped.
- Wardrobe and Daily have explicit loading/empty/error states.
- No critical regressions in navigation/auth flow.

### Decision
- **Pass** -> proceed to deeper core experience work.
- **Fail** -> fix UX friction and state handling before adding new features.

## Gate B (End of Week 6): Core Experience Validation

### Exit Criteria
- Guided first-run is live and visibly adopted.
- Daily explainability factors are shown on recommendation cards.
- Feedback and outfit-log actions are available and functional.
- Activation and trust metrics are measurable from events/logs.

### Decision
- **Pass** -> invest in differentiation layer.
- **Fail** -> prioritize adoption blockers over roadmap expansion.

## Gate C (End of Week 12): Differentiation Readiness

### Exit Criteria
- Wardrobe insight concepts are validated (capsule/gap framing).
- Explainability pattern is reusable and consistent.
- Team has evidence-backed direction: retention-first vs monetization-first.
- Next-quarter roadmap has KPI-linked hypotheses.

### Decision
- **Pass** -> scale growth experiments.
- **Fail** -> tighten core value loop and reduce scope.
